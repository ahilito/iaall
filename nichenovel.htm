Memrise a Niche Novel

studious recluse | intermediate-advanced


Learn through reading, encounter the difficult words before they get you.  This technique involves doing a Memrise course and reading a book simultaneously.  The language learner learns the difficult words before stumbling upon them in the book and through this tandem working, the language learner absorbs the words in a non-forced manner.


WHAT: The language learner chooses a book from the IAALL catalogue, currently being developed.  Each book in the IAALL catalogue will be coupled with a Memrise course.
