Theatre Performance

dare devil | beginner-intermediate-advanced


Give your language proficiency the ultimate test and boost through the creation of a monologue in the language you are learning.  Confront the reasons you are learning this language and the people you are going to speak it with in this extreme act of learning language.


WHAT: The Theatre Performance IAALL technique involves creating a monologue to be performed by the language learner in a theatre in the country of the language they are learning.  

HOW: The language learner finds a suitable collaborator who will work with them to create the write monologue, which they will then proceed to learn and eventually perform in front of an audience of locals.  The context of the performance can vary greatly of course depending on what the language learner is comfortable with.  It could be performed in a small theatre or even in a more informal setting.
